Walnut
The Walnut is a 150x100mm, high performance Carrier Board that is highly optimized for Internet of Things applications. It is pin-compatible with the all NutsBoard SoMs. It's including WAN, LAN, UART, CAN bus, USB and 6 GPIO's for Industrial control.
