Almond
The Almond SoM is a modular, small and powerful TI AM335X system on a chip (SoC) that includes a CPU, DDR3 memory, storage and Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. The module can be mounted onto a system of Carrier Board with DDR3-203 SODIMM pin design, enabling quick adoption and prototyping for the consumer and industrial IoT applications.
