About NutsBoard

NutsBoard focus on solutions based on embedded technologies. Our goal is to build connected platforms for a wide variety of industrial applications to you.

We are a group of experienced engineers. We not only offer full open source Software Development Kit and hardware boards, but also choosing System-on-Module approach that works with a carrier board design easily, it will reduce development time to market such as embedded platform, network or other IoT applications.

We aim to provide a powerful, long life time boards, not just producing a e-waste for our earth, so if you want to know more how Embedded Linux and Open Source can enhance your productivity, reduce cost and pollution at the same time, please join/contact us, change the world better together.

What can we do for you?

NutsBoard has much passion on embedded small board market, So our release including hardware, software OS runtime image, SDK source tar ball, and IoT framework API (2nd stage) to you, full open source of course, everyone can doing any applications using our solution.
