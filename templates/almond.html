Almond SoM

The Almond SoM is a modular, compact and powerful TI AM335X system on a chip (SoC) that includes a CPU, DDR3 memory, storage, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. The module can be mounted onto a system of Carrier Board with 204 pin SO-DIMM design, enabling quick adoption and prototyping for the consumer and industrial IoT applications.

Walnut is the high performance Ready-To-Go Carrier Board highly optimized for Internet of Things. It is pin-compatible with the 204 pin SO-DIMM NutsBoard SoMs. It included 1 WAN, 4 LANs, 2 UART, 2 USB, CAN bus and 8 GPIO's for industrial control.

Walnut PC-Almond is the max production box PC based on Almond SoM and Walnut Carrier Board in the metal case. The users can do fast development per specific requirement or we can provide engineering design services for such project.
